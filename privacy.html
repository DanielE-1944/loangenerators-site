Privacy Policy

We believe your financial data is yours alone. Here's exactly how we handle it.

👤 Who We Are

LoanGenerators ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website loangenerators.com and the LoanGenerators Android application available on Google Play. We provide free loan simulation and calculation tools to help users make informed financial decisions.

Contact: loangenerators@gmail.com

🔒 Data We Do Not Collect

Your loan data never leaves your device. All calculations — loan amounts, interest rates, payment schedules — are performed locally in your browser or app. We do not store, transmit, or have any access to the numbers you enter.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Loan amounts, interest rates, or repayment terms you enter
  • Names, addresses, or financial account details
  • Credit scores or banking information
  • Any personally identifiable information

📊 Data We May Collect Automatically

Like most websites, our hosting and analytics infrastructure may automatically collect limited technical data when you visit loangenerators.com:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Referring URL (how you found us)
  • Approximate geographic region (country/city level)
  • Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)

This data is anonymous, aggregated, and used solely to understand how users interact with our site so we can improve it. It cannot be used to identify you personally.

📱 Android App

The LoanGenerators Android app operates entirely offline. All loan simulations, amortization schedules, and comparisons are computed on your device. The app does not:

  • Require an account or registration
  • Send your financial inputs to any server
  • Access your contacts, location, or other device data
  • Display behaviorally targeted advertising

The app may be subject to Google Play's standard platform policies. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information about Google Play's data practices.

📢 Advertising

We may display advertisements on loangenerators.com through Google AdSense or similar ad networks. These services may use cookies and similar technologies to show relevant ads based on your browsing history across other websites. This is governed by Google's advertising policies.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings or by using the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out tool.

🍪 Cookies

Our website may use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics: to understand site usage patterns (anonymous)
  • Advertising: if AdSense is active, Google may set cookies to serve relevant ads
  • Preferences: to remember any display settings you choose

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

🔗 Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to external sites such as Google Play. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties and encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

👶 Children's Privacy

LoanGenerators is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

✏️ Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of LoanGenerators after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
